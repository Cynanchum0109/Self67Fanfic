### 结局文案（诗句，全部【自译待审】）

**1. 逃脱**
> ……我们开了一间小事务所。他还会头疼，我的手也时不时颤抖。我们仍从噩梦中惊醒，然后共享一个夜晚。生活就是这样开始的。

> …We opened a small office. He still gets headaches; my hands still tremble now and then. We still wake from nightmares — and then we share the night. That is how life begins.

**2. 存活（1v1）**
> 两人同行总会好些。/ 如果我们无法前进，/ 就让我们死在途中。/ 让我们死在一起。

> It is always better, two together. / If we cannot go on, / let us die on the way. / Let us die together.

**3. 兔子击杀驯鹿**
> 你可要痛快咬住我的脖子。/ 红血滴落。/ 草脏了，天近了。两颗眼珠上映现出彩虹。/ 我淡笑着，死了。/ 我一直等候着呢，这一刻。

> Bite deep into my neck, and make it clean. / Red blood drips down. / The grass is stained; the sky draws near. Rainbows shimmer in two eyes. / Smiling faintly, I died. / I have been waiting for it — this very moment.

**4. 驯鹿击杀兔子**
> 哈，我输了，你征服了我。我就把自己给你。/ 摄食我吧，我们当合为一体。/ 你要问我："这是最后的挣扎？假意屈服的计谋？"/ 我回答："吃吧，你早已涎水直流了。"

> Ha — I lost; you have conquered me. Then I give myself to you. / Devour me, and let us become one flesh. / You will ask: "A final struggle? A ruse of feigned surrender?" / I answer: "Eat. Your mouth has long been watering."

**5. 兔子存活（团灭）**
> 我们既不想预见结局，/ 又不能一起生存，—— / 哪怕是无休无止的爱，/ 哪怕是报以整个身心的恨。

> We neither wish to foresee the ending, / nor can we live as one — / be it a love that knows no end, / be it a hatred of the whole body and soul.

**6. 驯鹿存活（团灭）**
> 世间仍存在的幸福—— / 被所爱杀死。/ 谁怀着一个疲惫的灵魂，/ 闪烁着半疯半醒的幻想？—— / 你，还是我？

> There is still one happiness left in this world — / to be killed by the one you love. / Who bears a weary soul, / flickering with visions half-mad, half-waking? — / You, or I?
